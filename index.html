Who am I?

My name is Kai, and I am a freelance graphic designer, sometimes photographer, and other times an illustrator. In other words, I am an artist. I have spent the past few years mastering graphic design using a software called Inkscape, and I create everything from simple logos, to t-shirt designs, to full-blown illustrations, and everything in between. Commissions for artwork are ALWAYS open, and my email is always open for business inquiries at kaimereesewelsh@gmail.com. If clean, quality vector art and illustration is what you want, then you've come to the right place!
